FAQ

Common Questions

Everything business owners ask before getting started.

Do my staff need to do anything extra?
No. After a short 30–45 minute training on how to enter customer details, the system handles everything automatically — sending messages, collecting responses, and routing reviews.
What if a customer is unhappy?
Unhappy customers are routed to a private feedback form instead of Google. Their complaints come directly to you, not the public. This protects your star rating while still giving you actionable feedback. (Google review link remains accessible on the form for full compliance).
Will this feel spammy to customers?
Not at all. Customers receive one polite WhatsApp message after their visit, and one gentle reminder if they don't respond. They can opt out anytime. Messages are personalized with their name and your business name.
What happens after the 30-day pilot?
If you're happy with the results, the system continues at ₹4,999/month — all-inclusive. No lock-in contracts. You can cancel anytime and the service runs until the end of your current billing period.
Is my customer data safe?
Yes. All data is stored securely on Google's infrastructure. Customer personal data is automatically anonymized after 30 days. We never share or sell your data to anyone. Customers can request deletion anytime.
How quickly can I see results?
Setup takes 7 days. First reviews start coming in within 48 hours of going live. Most businesses see a noticeable jump in their Google review count within the first two weeks.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 April 2026  |  Last Updated: 15 April 2026

JN AI Automations ("we", "us", "our") operates the website jnaiautomations.com and provides CEDE — an automated WhatsApp-based Google review collection system for local businesses in India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA 2023).

1. Our Role Under DPDPA 2023

Under DPDPA 2023, JN AI Automations acts as a Data Processor — we process personal data on behalf of the businesses that use our service. The business you visited (the salon, clinic, or retail store) is the Data Fiduciary — they are responsible for obtaining your consent and instructing us on how your data is used. If you have questions about why your data was collected, contact the business you visited first.

2. Information We Collect

From our clients (business owners): Business name, owner name, phone number, email address, Google Business Profile details, WhatsApp Business Account details, and payment information.

From our clients' customers (End Customers): Name, phone number, visit details (date, time, service received, staff member), feedback rating (1–5 stars), feedback text if submitted, opt-out status, and a consent record (timestamp, method, and staff name). This data is entered into our system by the business's staff — we do not collect it directly from End Customers without the business's involvement.

Automatically: Website usage data including IP address, browser type, and pages visited, collected via Google Analytics (GA4).

3. How We Use Information

We use the information collected solely to: set up and operate the automated WhatsApp review collection system for our clients; send one WhatsApp message and one follow-up reminder to customers after their visit; route positive feedback (4–5 stars) to a Google Review link; route negative feedback (1–3 stars) to a private feedback form sent to the business owner; generate performance reports for our clients; process payments and manage subscriptions; and improve our services. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering this service.

4. Data Retention

Message logs and visit records: Retained for 12 months from creation, then aggregated into anonymous statistics and permanently deleted.

Customer personal records (name, phone): Retained while the client's subscription is active, plus 30 days after cancellation, then cryptographically redacted — name and phone number are permanently scrambled and cannot be recovered.

Opt-out records: Retained indefinitely as a non-reversible flag. We must retain your opt-out status to ensure we never re-message you.

Feedback form submissions: Retained for 12 months, then deleted.

System logs: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted.

5. Data Security

All data is stored on DigitalOcean's secured infrastructure in Bangalore, India. Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Our database encrypts data at rest by default. Our application server is protected by Cloudflare's Web Application Firewall and DDoS mitigation — no application ports are publicly exposed. Strict access controls limit database and server access to authorized JN AI Automations personnel only. Each business's customer data is logically separated — one business cannot access another's data. Automated daily database backups with 7-day retention are maintained. All API credentials are stored in an encrypted credential manager, never in plain text.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with any third party for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose. Data is shared only with the sub-processors listed in Section 4 above, solely for the purpose of operating our service.

7. Data Residency

All personal data under our direct control is stored within India — Bangalore (DigitalOcean) and India data centers (Zoho). This satisfies the data localization requirements of DPDPA 2023. Note: WhatsApp message delivery is handled by Meta's global infrastructure, which is outside our direct control.

8. Your Rights

Right to opt out: Reply STOP to any WhatsApp message from our system at any time. We will immediately and permanently stop all messaging to your number. Variants including "stop", "unsubscribe", "opt out", "cancel", and "do not message me" are also recognized.

Right to erasure: Contact the business you visited to request deletion of your data. If unresolved, contact us directly at admin@jnaiautomations.com. Upon a valid erasure request, we will cryptographically redact your name and phone number from our system — this permanently destroys your personal identifiers while preserving anonymous aggregate data.

Right to access: Request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.

Right to grievance redressal: If your request is not handled satisfactorily, you may approach the Data Protection Board of India at meity.gov.in.

9. Client Responsibilities

Our clients (business owners) are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from their customers before entering data into our system. Our intake form includes a mandatory checkbox confirming the customer has consented to receive a WhatsApp feedback message and is 18 years or older.

Anti-Gating Compliance: Our system routes unhappy customers to a private feedback form, but does not block or hide the Google review option. Both options are always presented to the customer, ensuring full compliance with Google's review policies and DPDPA 2023.

10. Children's Data

Our system is not intended for processing personal data of individuals under 18 years of age. The intake form includes a mandatory confirmation that the customer is 18 or older. If you believe data of a minor has been entered without proper parental consent, contact us immediately at admin@jnaiautomations.com and we will investigate and take appropriate action including data erasure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes (such as adding a new sub-processor or changing data retention periods), we will notify the businesses using our service so they can inform their customers. The "Last Updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
